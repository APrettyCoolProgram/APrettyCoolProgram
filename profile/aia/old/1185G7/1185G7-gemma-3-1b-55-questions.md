**1. If a train leaves the station at 3 PM and travels at 60 miles per hour, how far will it have traveled by 6 PM?**

* From 3 PM to 6 PM is 3 hours.
* Distance = Speed x Time = 60 mph * 3 hours = 180 miles

**Answer:** The train will have traveled 180 miles by 6 PM.

**2. If a rectangle has a length of 10 units and a width of 5 units, what is its area?**

* Area = Length x Width = 10 units * 5 units = 50 square units.

**Answer:** The area of the rectangle is 50 square units.

**3. If a person has 3 apples and gives away 2, how many apples do they have left?**

* Start with: 3 apples
* Give away: 2 apples
* Remaining: 3 - 2 = 1 apple

**Answer:** The person has 1 apple left.

**4. If a car travels at a speed of 50 miles per hour for 2 hours, how far will it have traveled?**

* Distance = Speed x Time = 50 mph * 2 hours = 100 miles

**Answer:** The car will have traveled 100 miles.

**5. If a box contains 10 red balls and 5 blue balls, what is the probability of randomly selecting a red ball?**

* Total number of balls: 10 + 5 = 15
* Number of red balls: 10
* Probability (Red) = (Number of Red Balls) / (Total Number of Balls) = 10/15 = 2/3  ≈ 0.67

**Answer:** The probability of selecting a red ball is approximately 2/3 or 67%.

**6.  If a person has $100 and spends $30, how much money do they have left?**

* Start with: $100
* Spend: $30
* Money Left = $100 - $30 = $70

**Answer:** The person has $70 left.

**7. If a clock shows 3:00 PM, what time will it show after 4 hours?**

* Add 4 hours to 3:00 PM: 3:00 PM + 4 hours = 7:00 PM

**Answer:** It will show 7:00 PM.

**8. If a person has 5 siblings and each sibling has 2 children, how many nieces and nephews does the person have?**

* There are 5 siblings.
* Each sibling has 2 children, so there are 5 * 2 = 10 children.
* Nieces and Nephews:  The child from the first sibling is a niece/nephew, and so on. So, it's 10 + 1 = 11 nieces and nephews.

**Answer:** The person has 11 nieces and nephews.

**9. If a rectangle has a length of 8 units and a width of 4 units, what is its perimeter?**

* Perimeter = 2 * (Length + Width) = 2 * (8 units + 4 units) = 2 * 12 units = 24 units.

**Answer:** The perimeter is 24 units.

**10. If a person has 10 marbles and gives away 3, how many marbles do they have left?**

* Start with: 10 marbles
* Give away: 3 marbles
* Remaining: 10 - 3 = 7 marbles

**Answer:** The person has 7 marbles left.
